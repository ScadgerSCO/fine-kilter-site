This notice will be completed and reviewed before the site goes live. It isn't finished, and pretending otherwise would be a poor start for a practice built on straight answers.

What it will cover, plainly

The commitments already made elsewhere on this site hold: your details go to me and nowhere else, there's no mailing list, and no follow-up sequence.
